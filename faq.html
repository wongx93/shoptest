what is resin?

Resin is a thermosetting medium. When epoxy resin is mixed with a hardening agent in a correct ratio in a workspace with the right temperature, a chemical reaction occurs that begins the curing process into an inert and solid plastic.

how do i care for my resin pieces?

Pieces are water-resistant, but not waterproof. Please do not submerge your jewelry in water for extended periods of time. To clean, wipe with a damp cloth. Surfaces are slightly porous, avoid spraying chemicals/alcohol-based products (hairsprays, perfumes, nail polish removers) on pieces to avoid absorbency and deterioration.

Resin can scratch and break when excessive force is applied. Treat your pieces with love and kindness!

is resin food-safe?

Fully cured resin is generally regarded as food-safe and can be used for brief-use direct food contact.

can the planter pots be used for candles?

While planters make great candle holders, pouring candle wax directly in the containers is not advised.
