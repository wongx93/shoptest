about me.

I am an NYC-based UX designer with experience in digital publisher ad operations with a certificate in UX/UI from Columbia University Engineering and a B.S. in Media Culture & Communication from New York University, minoring in Business of Entertainment, Media, and Technology.

I have a background in display and video ad implementation & QA across desktop, mobile, tablet, and streaming channels for The Weather Channel and NHL digital properties.

I am fundamentally centered around design-centered thinking methods through established user research, definition, prototyping and testing best practices with an ability to synthesize and evaluate user success via quantifiable metrics, reporting, and analytics.

My background in advertising allows me to harmoniously bridge usability needs with broader business objectives and client goals, along with a technical grasp for product and sales workflows.
