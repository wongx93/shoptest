shipping policy

All orders are processed within 1-3 business days (excluding weekends and holidays) after receiving your order confirmation email. You will receive another notification when your order has shipped.

domestic & international shipping rates & estimates

Shipping charges for your order will be calculated and displayed at checkout. International shipping prices vary by territory. See shipping choices by carrier at check-out.
